About

I grew up pretty engineering-brained. I studied CS and work as a software developer day-to-day.

As an impressionable kid on the internet, I always wanted to make games. I eventually made a few with friends in game jams!

As an Asian kid, I took piano lessons and wanted to make my own video-game-inspired music. I eventually learned how with pxtone and a wonderful internet community around it!

I've been a furry for a while but only learned to draw and become active post-pandemic.

Some works

  • pxtone collab (2021) - A multiplayer piano roll editor based off the pxtone sound engine.

    Inspired by drawpile. Made during the pandemic - I really wanted to do a collab but was too engineering brained and tried to solve my human problems with technical solutions. Still, turned out to be useful to a lot of people, even just as a standalone editor. Received huge support from the pxtone community!

  • pxtone web (2018) - A site for sharing, visualizing, and listening to pxtone music.

    Inspired by picotune. Made in my last year of college when I had a light courseload. I wanted to make it easier for pxtone musicians to share music with each other. Also incredible support from the pxtone community!

  • Vikings (2017) - Global Game Jam entry.

    Platform fighter where you charge shockwaves to knock others out of the ring. Made with friends in college; I did the programming and a large part of the design. Awarded 2nd at the Microsoft NYC site!

  • Assorted music (2015 -) -

    I was a composer for some friends' Touhou game jam entries (Suwa Blaster and Fishy Dreams). I did a bunch of one-hour compos on battle of the bits over the pandemic. Most of my sketches are on ptweb, with my bigger works on Soundcloud. (A lot of them are part of pxtone community albums called pxtunes, do check them out!)
